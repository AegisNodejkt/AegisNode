## How to become a maintainer

Maintainers are key contributors who have demonstrated a long-term commitment to the project.
If you are interested in becoming a maintainer, please:

1. Contribute consistently (code, documentation, issue triage).
2. Follow the Code of Conduct.
3. Express your interest in an issue or by emailing existing maintainers.
